# Physics-Aware Dynamic 3DGS — Evidence Matrix

> **As of 2026-07-12.** Companion to [Physics-Aware Dynamic 3DGS Literature Review](3dgs/physics-aware-dynamic-3dgs-literature-review.md). This matrix contains 78 unique works: 72 from the 3DGS era and six older roots selected for dynamic radiance fields, non-rigid reconstruction, SLAM, and continuum physics.

---

## How to Read This Matrix

- **PR** means the publication venue was verified from an official arXiv record, proceedings record, or the paper itself. **PP** means a technical preprint whose venue was not verified in this pass.
- A paper can carry several tags: **Dyn** (dynamic scene), **Def** (deformation), **SLAM**, **Phys** (mechanics or physical image formation), **Comp** (compression/efficiency), **R-Int** (robot/contact interaction), and **H-Int** (human language/editing interaction).
- “Boundary” records what the paper does not establish for this review. It is a scope assessment, not necessarily a limitation claimed verbatim by the authors.
- Common evidence fields are reconstruction quality (PSNR/SSIM/LPIPS), trajectory error (ATE/RPE), rate–distortion (bits or MB versus image quality), throughput/FPS, and task success.
- Every title links to the primary arXiv record. Code is marked only when an official paper/project record or the existing seed bibliography exposed it; “not verified” does not mean unavailable.
